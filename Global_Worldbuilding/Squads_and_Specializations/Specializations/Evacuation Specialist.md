# Evacuation Specialist

## Description
The expert in "Getting Out." They act as the retreat coordinators, skilled in setting rear-guard traps, creating smoke screens, and identifying the fastest escape routes.

## Operational Variants
*   **Extraction Context:** Focuses on *Speed*. Moving a target (who may be injured or slow) rapidly across terrain.
*   **Assassination Context:** Focuses on *Misdirection*. Creating false trails to confuse pursuers after the hit is confirmed.
