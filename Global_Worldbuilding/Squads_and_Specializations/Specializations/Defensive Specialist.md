# Defensive Specialist

## Description
Masters of Barrier Ninjutsu (Kekkaijutsu). They create energy domes and seals to block incoming attacks.

## Operational Variants
*   **Personal Defense:** Focuses on *Shielding*. Protecting a specific person (VIP/Medic).
*   **Area Defense:** Focuses on *Fortification*. Protecting a camp or building from bombardment.
