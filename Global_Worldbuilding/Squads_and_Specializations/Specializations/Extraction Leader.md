# Extraction Leader

## Description
A squad leader specialized in high-stress decision making. They must balance the safety of the Asset (VIP/Intel) with the safety of the squad.

## Operational Variants
*   **Rescue Ops:** Focuses on *Asset Health*. Ensuring the prisoner/VIP survives the trip.
*   **Intel Ops:** Focuses on *Asset Security*. Ensuring the scroll/item is not destroyed or recaptured.
