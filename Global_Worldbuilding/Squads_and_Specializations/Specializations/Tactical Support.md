# Tactical Support

## Description
The "Buffer." This broad specialization covers shinobi who use jutsu to aid their allies directly, whether through stamina transfer, minor healing, or sensory linking.

## Operational Variants
*   **Combat Support:** Focuses on *Battle Rhythm*. Timing jutsu to cover an ally's opening.
*   **Strategic Support:** Focuses on *Analysis*. analyzing enemy patterns and relaying weaknesses to the squad leader.
