# Frontline Fighter

## Description
The "Tank" or "Vanguard." These shinobi possess high durability and physical strength. Their primary role is to draw aggro, engage the main enemy force, and hold the line.

## Operational Variants
*   **Assault Context:** Focuses on *Breakthrough*. Smashing through enemy defenses to open a path for the rest of the squad.
*   **Guard Context:** Focuses on *Immovability*. Standing ground to prevent any enemy from passing a specific perimeter.
