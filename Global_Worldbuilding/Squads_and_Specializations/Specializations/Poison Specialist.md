# Poison Specialist

## Description
Experts in chemistry and biology. They create lethal toxins for weapons and antidotes for allies.

## Operational Variants
*   **Combat Context:** Focuses on *Debilitation*. Using gas or coated blades to slow or paralyze enemies.
*   **Medical Context:** Focuses on *Antidote Synthesis*. Analyzing enemy poisons to create a cure on the fly.
