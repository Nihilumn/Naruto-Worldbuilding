# Disruption Specialist

## Description
Agents of chaos. They specialize in Genjutsu, blinding techniques, and sensory jamming. Their goal is to break the enemy's chain of command and situational awareness.

## Operational Variants
*   **Battlefield Context:** Focuses on *Mass Confusion*. Affecting large groups to break formations.
*   **Sabotage Context:** Focuses on *System Disruption*. Disabling sensory wards or communication arrays.
