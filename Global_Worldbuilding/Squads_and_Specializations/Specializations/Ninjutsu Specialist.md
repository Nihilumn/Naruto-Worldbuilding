# Ninjutsu Specialist

## Description
The "Artillery." These shinobi have large chakra reserves and mastery over multiple elemental natures. They provide ranged firepower and versatility.

## Operational Variants
*   **Assault Context:** Focuses on *Area of Effect (AoE)*. Wiping out clusters of enemies.
*   **Duelist Context:** Focuses on *Counter-play*. Having the right element to counter a specific enemy's jutsu.
