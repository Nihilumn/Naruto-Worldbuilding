# Precision Striker

## Description
Combatants who focus on quality over quantity. Rather than wide-area destruction, they utilize high-speed, high-penetration techniques to target vital points (Tenketsu, arteries, structural weak points).

## Operational Variants
*   **Assassination Context:** Focuses on *Lethality*. One-hit-kill techniques designed to end a fight instantly.
*   **Disabling Context:** Focuses on *Crippling*. Severing tendons or chakra points to capture a target alive.
