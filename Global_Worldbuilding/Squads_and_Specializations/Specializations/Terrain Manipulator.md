# Terrain Manipulator

## Description
Specialists in Earth and Water release who physically alter the battlefield. Unlike Demolitions experts who destroy, these specialists build and shape.

## Operational Variants
*   **Battlefield Control:** Focuses on *Zoning*. Creating walls and pits to force enemies into kill zones.
*   **Defensive Context:** Focuses on *Fortification*. Rapidly building bunkers or trenches.
