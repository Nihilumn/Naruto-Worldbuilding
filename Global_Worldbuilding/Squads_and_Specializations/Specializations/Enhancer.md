# Enhancer

## Description
A specific type of support ninja who casts "buffs" and carries enhancement items. They increase the physical parameters (speed, strength, durability) of their allies.

## Operational Variants
*   **Vanguard Support:** Focuses on *Strength/Durability*. Making the Frontline Fighter harder to kill.
*   **Striker Support:** Focuses on *Speed/Reaction*. Making the Precision Striker faster than the enemy.
