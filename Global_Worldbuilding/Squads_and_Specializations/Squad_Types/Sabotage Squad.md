# Sabotage Squad

## Overview
Sabotage Squads operate to cripple enemy infrastructure. Whether destroying bridges, supply depots, or communication arrays, their goal is to weaken the enemy's ability to wage war without engaging their main forces.

## Standard Composition
*   **Demolitions Expert:** Specializes in using explosive devices and other means to destroy targets. They are skilled in setting traps and causing structural damage.
*   **Stealth Expert:** Gains access to enemy installations or areas where sabotage is needed. They use stealth and deception to move undetected.
*   **Disruption Specialist:** Focuses on disabling enemy resources, such as communication lines, supplies, or equipment. They ensure that the sabotage has a long-lasting impact on the enemy's operations.
