# Medic Squad

## Overview
Focused entirely on health and biology, these squads are deployed to run field hospitals or mitigate biological warfare. They are high-value targets for enemies and usually operate behind the main lines.

## Standard Composition
*   **Medic:** Specializes in healing injuries and treating various ailments. They are skilled in medical ninjutsu, capable of performing surgeries, healing wounds, and ensuring the squad's overall health during missions.
*   **Poison Specialist:** Expert in the creation and use of poisons. They can concoct a variety of toxins for use in combat, whether for coating weapons or creating poisonous clouds. They are also skilled in antidote preparation and can neutralize enemy poisons.
*   **Defensive Specialist:** Focuses on creating protective barriers and shields to safeguard the squad. They use defensive jutsu to block attacks, create safe zones for medical treatment, and provide a defensive line against enemy forces.
