# Diplomat Squad

## Overview
A non-combative unit focused on political maneuvering, treaty negotiation, and alliance building. While they may be escorted by Guard Squads, the members of this squad are selected for intellect and charisma rather than combat prowess.

## Standard Composition
*   **Diplomatic Leader:** The primary negotiator and representative. They engage in discussions, form alliances, and handle political matters.
*   **Advisor:** Provides counsel and strategic advice. They understand the political landscape and assist the leader in making informed decisions.
*   **Logistics Coordinator:** Manages the practical aspects of the diplomatic mission, such as travel arrangements, communications, and security. They ensure the smooth operation of the diplomatic efforts.
