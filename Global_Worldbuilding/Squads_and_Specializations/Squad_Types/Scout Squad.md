# Scout Squad

## Overview
The eyes and ears of the village. Scout Squads specialize in long-range data collection and tracking. They avoid combat whenever possible, as their primary objective is to return with information.

## Standard Composition
*   **Tracker:** Specializes in locating and following targets. They use sensory abilities, tracking skills, and knowledge of terrain to gather information on enemy movements.
*   **Reconnaissance Expert:** Gathers intelligence on enemy positions, strengths, and weaknesses. They use observation and data collection techniques to provide valuable insights for planning.
*   **Communications Specialist:** Maintains contact with the main forces or command center. They relay information quickly and accurately to ensure timely decisions and actions.
