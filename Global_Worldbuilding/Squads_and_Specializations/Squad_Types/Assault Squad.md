# Assault Squad

## Overview
The backbone of any military offensive, Assault Squads are designed for direct confrontation. They possess high damage output and durability, capable of breaking enemy lines and securing objectives through force.

## Standard Composition
*   **Frontline Fighter:** Specializes in close combat and is typically the strongest physically. They engage the enemy directly and often serve as the squad's primary offensive force.
*   **Ninjutsu Specialist:** Utilizes a wide range of ninjutsu techniques to provide versatile attacks and support. They can handle various combat situations and adapt to different enemy tactics.
*   **Tactical Support:** Provides healing, defensive barriers, or supplementary abilities to enhance the effectiveness of the squad. They ensure the team stays in fighting condition and can also disrupt enemy strategies.
